Monthly Newsletter Creation Process

We have a systematic process setup, which lives inside of a Basecamp project. Each month I'll create a new project, which has a templated todo list of tasks that need to be carried out at specific times of the month. Such as, a mid-month call for content or assigning the display priority of events.

I'll then code and upload the newsletter into MailChimp . We have an approval process in place where I send out a test email so we can have multiple eyes look over it, before I schedule it out to be sent at times where we believe we'll receive the largest amount of engagement from our constituents.

2015 Redesign

Upon joining the Jacksonville Chapter Board in 2014, I was tasked with updating the monthly communications newsletter. The redesign brought forth a single-column modular layout, full-width images, bold new accent colors, and a vast reduction of text. Allowing it to act more as inspiration and a friendly upcoming event reminder, directing the audience to the website; where they can find full event details.
