Bistro23

American Style Bistro (Student Project)

Bistro 23 is a fine-dining restaurant with an american cuisine menu. Located within the center of the screen are the menu options providing fragment identifier links to other sections of this one-page site, as well as the contrasting logo for brand recognition.

Above the Fold

The entire screen is filled with large-scale imagery of the restaurant, on a rotation timer.

Menu

As you scroll down, the menu fixates to the top of the browser. Once you reach the interior sections, you will find a an accordion-style menu to better organize the full list of menu items; this helps the user not feel overwhelmed with options.
