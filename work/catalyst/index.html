Catalyst

Innovation Workshops

Catalyst workshops facilitated a creative, safe, and exciting environment for teams to come together to define and prioritize specific problems. Our exercises brought out the voices in teams by generating groundbreaking ideas. We then experimented and evaluated the solutions to develop a project plan to implement the best results for the business.

Each session included fun teambuilding activities which led to real business outcomes.

Interest Landing Page

Catalyst was unique for Ignite . Unlike the rest of our products, it was not technology reliant. The only necessary digital piece was the landing page for new sign-ups. The webpage would often be included in promotional emails, as it helped employees convince company executives to make an investment in their team and themselves.

My original task for this site was to assist with front-end development, however, when building the page I felt a vital piece was missing. Instead of a static photo of the space, why not capture and share a bit of the experience?

After making a suggestion to create a looping video as the background, I was nominated for the task and we shot our next workshop to gather footage.
