Combofonts

Webfont Combination Application

Combofonts matches and recommends webfont pairs. It's a personal project I worked on, alongside my product design apprenticeship.

The application asks a series of questions based on the tone and mood of the context the text will be displayed in, provides you with a few combinations of options, and then gives you the markup to copy & paste into any codebase.

For the MVP I started with the Google font library , as it's a great free resource.

  • Design & Front-End: Jason Kellum
  • Development: Jason Cummings

In-App Screens

Acting as a educational playground for me, this was one of my first experiences with a Ruby on Rails project. It also gave me an excuse to take a deeper dive into typography on the web.

The largest obstacle was finding a way to provide quality results, while asking the fewest amount of questions necessary.

At the very least, this was a great product design exercise, allowing me to practice the techniques and learnings from my KYN apprenticeship.
