Ignite

Innovation lab for Adecco Group

Ignite partnered with Adecco business units and sister companies to develop new technology to drive efficiency and bring new products to market and help people work.

The company was in need of a new marketing website to define who we were and how we solve problems. A team was assembled and we got to work.

2015 Marketing Site

In just two years from Ignite's launch, we outgrew the capabilities of what our previous one-page marketing website could provide. With a goal of raising brand awareness and increasing product adoption rate, we aimed to make more of our information and resources available online.

Since this wasn't one of the organization's prioritized products, our entire design team collaborated to meet the project deadlines. My role on the team was to build a great codebase any of us could use and expand upon.

Codebase

This Ruby on Rails project was built with HAML and SASS, running with Bourbon and Neat. I typically work split screen, allowing me to reference both my HTML and CSS or JS simultaneously. I've been using Atom as my text editor recently, as it runs nicely alongside GitHub's version management.
