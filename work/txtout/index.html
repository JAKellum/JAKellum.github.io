TxtOut

Attendance Tracking Tool

Created to relieve a business pain-point, TxtOut replaced an outdated voice-mail center used by Nike and Ebay manufacturing warehouses.

Associates can call-out and, for the first time, text-out tardy or absent. For the staffing agency, it frees associates from manually transcribing voicemails and provides them with searchable, digital attendance reports.

In-App Screens

Upon returning from the end of the year holidays, I was apart of a team that was formed to create TxtOut. It was a fast-pace, 3 month initiative with the goal of creating an interface for a well-preforming, quickly hacked together system.

The interface consisted of a general dashboard which displayed call-outs for individual branches, along with authentication and admin pages.

This project continued on to become one of Ignite 's most successful tools, later receiving a larger budget and timeframe to add additional features.
